History of Equilati

Founded in 1992 in Várzea Paulista, Equilati was born from the dream of its owner, João Finkler, to create high-quality and functional equipment for various industrial sectors. Initially focused on the common challenges faced by a company in its early stages, Equilati overcame obstacles and gradually developed.

The company specialized in the manufacturing of industrial equipment, especially for dairies, juices, eggs, beers, and soft drinks, standing out for proprietary technology and customized projects that meet the specific needs of each client.

With over 30 years of experience, Equilati expanded its operations to the international market, serving clients on three continents and establishing successful partnerships. The company is proud to have over 2,000 satisfied customers and offers both remote and on-site support.

Equilati's journey is marked by excellence and the ability to innovate and adapt to market changes, always committed to delivering equipment that efficiently ensures the quality its clients require.

Mission

To manufacture innovative products with quality and affordability for our clients, connecting technology with human care.



Vision

To be a reference in the manufacturing of pasteurizers and heat exchangers, recognized for the quality, innovation, and technology of our equipment in both national and international markets.

Values

  • Innovation: We encourage creativity and the continuous pursuit of innovative solutions.
  • Ethics and Transparency: We act with integrity and respect in all our relationships with employees, clients, and partners.
  • Sustainability: We value and promote practices that contribute to environmental preservation and the development of a fairer and more balanced society.
  • Human Care: We put people at the center of everything we do, offering care and support that respect individuality and promote overall well-being.
