Brazed Heat Exchangers

Brazed heat exchangers are highly efficient devices for heat transfer. They are compact, maintenance-free, and can withstand high temperatures and pressures.

These heat exchangers operate through corrugated metal plates that create alternating channels for two fluids to flow in countercurrent. The brazed sealing around the plates ensures pressure resistance and efficiency in heat transfer.

Main applications:

  • Heating
  • Cooling
