What tubular pasteurization systems are and their purpose

The tubular pasteurization unit from Equilati Equipment is a complete system used in the food industry to pasteurize liquids such as ice cream bases, yogurt mixes, juices, pulps, sauces, craft beers, and other higher-viscosity products. It consists of stainless steel tubes through which the product flows, being heated and cooled in a controlled manner.

Advantages of the tubular system

This type of pasteurizer is ideal for continuous and automated production lines, ensuring quality and safety with minimal contamination risk.

  • High thermal efficiency and food safety.
  • Allows pasteurization of viscous products and those with small particles, operating at higher pressures and temperatures.
  • Durability: built with certified A270 stainless steel tubes in 304 and 316L, resistant to corrosion.
  • Flexibility: allows precise temperature and time adjustments.
  • Preserves sensory characteristics: maintains food flavor, aroma, and texture without compromising quality.
